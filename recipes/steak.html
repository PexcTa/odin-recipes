Steak Recipe - At Home!

Description

But you are probably here only for the steak recipe, so let me show you how to get this bad boy all tasty a juicy while only armed with an oven, a range, and a cast iron pan. Please don't do This in some piece of Tefal bullshit.

Ingredients

    You will need:
  • A Steak
    • New York Strip or Ribeye are good
    • Sirloin will work great as well
    • Fillet Mignon might be a bit hard to get right with this method
  • Salt and Pepper
  • a Cast Iron Pan

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees C and place the pan inside
    • how do I type a degrees symbol in HTML?
  2. Pre-season your steak with salt and pepper on both sides, pat dry
  3. Take the super hot pan out, put it on high heat, sear steak on one side for 1-2 min depending on thickness
  4. Flip and sear on the other side for the same amount of time
  5. Ready! Enjoy with some nice side like spinach!
